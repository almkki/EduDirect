About EduDirect

Welcome to EduDirect!

We're a team of passionate university students who wanted to create something truly helpful for students like us. This website is actually our graduation project, built with the goal of guiding students through academic decisions and career planning in a smarter, easier way.

We know how overwhelming it can be to choose the right path—so we designed EduDirect to make things simpler. Our AI-powered platform analyzes your skills, interests, and academic performance to provide personalized recommendations that help you make informed decisions about your future.
